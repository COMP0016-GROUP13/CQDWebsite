Our team has been given the responsibility of further extending this project to allow for more functionalities, which can hugely benefit clinicians at NHS Wales. REQUIREMENTS
The requirements we acquire from semi-structured interviews with pseudo-users help us in the direction of our project development. Our targetted users are clinians and also head of departments of hospitals under NHS Wales. They can be categorised as follows:
TIME MANAGEMENT

Saves a lot of clinicians' valuable time by having an easily-navigable interface for self-evaluation

SUPERVISION

Allows Head-of-Departments to supervise clinicians for continued improvement in care quality provided

USABILITY

Multiple dashboard feature which allows all departments within the hospital to utilise this interface
