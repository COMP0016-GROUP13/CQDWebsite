About Care Quality Dashboard (CQD)

Large influx of patients coming in to receive treatment, especially during the Covid-19 pandemic. Consequently, it is imperative for NHSW to provide the best quality of treatment to patients as outlined by the Welsh Government's Health and Care Standards. In line with the constant need of top-notch care services, our team has come up with an idea on how to help clinicians evaluate themselves for keeping their care quality in check. That is how Care Quality Dashboard was created.

Through the integration of our system into the NHSW, clinicians are able to continuously self-evaluate and measure the quality of their services against the NHSW standards. On top of that, Head-of-Departments, too, are able to supervise fellow clinicians in an effort to ensure constant quality treatment. Currently, we can proudly say that our system is currently in use in the NHS Wales and we are continuously trying to improve on the pre-existing interface.
